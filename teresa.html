Mariupol evacuation-window building damage lab

Compares one fixed 2021 baseline with imagery nearest each evacuation period, calculates visual change inside OpenStreetMap building footprints, fills only high-change building footprints in red and prints the detected damaged-building percentage directly on the after image. Intact buildings remain unmarked, and no circles are drawn. The result is a research screening layer—not verified damage.
